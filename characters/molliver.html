Magic Items

✦ Rope of Climbing
50' magical rope. Climbs, descends, and moves on mental command at 10'/round. Can tie and untie knots by itself. Holds up to 3,000 cn (300 lbs). Particularly useful for a thief who already has 93% Climb Walls — the rope covers the remaining 7% and handles vertical obstacles entirely hands-free.
✦ Ring of Protection +2
+2 to AC and all saving throws. With Leather +1 and this ring, AC improves to 5 — important since thieves cannot use shields.
