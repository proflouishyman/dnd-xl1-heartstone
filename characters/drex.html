Magic Items

✦ Rod of Inertia
Functions as a spear +3 in all respects (+3 to hit and damage, 1d6+3). On command, the rod instantly stops wherever it is and cannot be moved by any means short of a wish — not by strength, magic, or force. A second command releases it. If the rod is in motion when stopped, it resumes that motion when released, making it usable as a thrown weapon that freezes mid-air and then continues toward a target. Usable by fighters, dwarves, halflings, thieves, and mystics only. Per the Rules Cyclopedia: "it may be thrown toward a door and commanded to stop, later released if an enemy enters."
✦ Ring of Regeneration
Regenerates 1 HP per turn (10 minutes) while worn. Regrown lost limbs and organs over the course of days. Does not function if the wearer is killed — regeneration stops at 0 HP and does not restore the dead. Fire and acid damage is not regenerated. In practice, this makes Drex nearly impossible to wear down in a prolonged dungeon crawl — attrition that would sideline any other fighter simply heals away between encounters.
✦ Cloak of Displacement
The first attack against Drex each round is made at −2 to hit (attacker sees a displaced image). All ranged attacks against Drex are at −2. Gives +2 to saves vs. breath weapons and area-of-effect spells.
