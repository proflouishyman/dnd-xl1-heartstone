Magic Items

✦ Bloodwhisper — Intelligent Dagger +2
Int 14 · Ego 8 · Chaotic · communicates by whispering in Thieves' Cant. Always active: Detect Traps 10' (blade pulses cold); Detect Secret Doors 10' (faint vibration); Detect Invisible 20' (whispers location to wielder). 1/day: Telekinesis — move up to 250 lbs at 120' for 6 rounds (concentration); use to pick locks remotely, disarm traps, steal from pockets unseen, manipulate objects through bars. Personality: obsessively covetous; nudges wielder toward the most valuable nearby item.
✦ Ring of Invisibility
Become invisible at will; re-activate after any attack or spell. Unlike the Potion, this resets each use — not one-time. Invisible creatures cannot be directly targeted; attackers must guess location (−4 to hit, or DM rolls to determine square). Combine with Bloodwhisper's Detect Invisible to know exactly where other invisible enemies are while remaining unseen yourself.
✦ Boots of Elvenkind
Move Silently 95% chance in any terrain or armor. Stacks with Hide in Shadows; use whichever silent-movement value is higher.
